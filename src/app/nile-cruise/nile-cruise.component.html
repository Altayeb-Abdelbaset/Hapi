Source of life for people, animals and plants and the god of the Nile. Described as the reason for life and peace, the destroyer of darkness, the irrigator of the fields of Ra and the creator of grain. Prayers for glorification are many. It represents the fascination and power of the Nile in many myths.

Hapi V sail across the Nile with ca. 700 tons, a length of 72 meters and a heigth of ca. 14 meters. Each for 8 hours. Around 80 employees are responsible for your comfort, your safety and care .. The Nile Cruise travels between Luxor and Aswan covering a distance of 230 km one way and takes 15 tons / Liters of gas.
